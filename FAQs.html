What is the program about?
Answer: The Dunamis Skill Acquisition Program is a training program designed to equip participants with various skills and knowledge to enhance their employability and entrepreneurial capabilities.
What are the qualifications for participating in the program?
Answer: The program is open to individuals who are interested in acquiring new skills and knowledge, regardless of their educational background or prior experience.
What are the requirements for registration?
Answer: To register for the program, you need to fill out an online application form and provide the necessary documentation, such as Dunamis Membership certificate, and Dunamis HomeCell details.
What skills are offered in the program?
Answer: The program offers a variety of skills, including but not limited to, Catering, Fashion design, Interior Decoration, Hair Styling, ICT(Web & Graphics Design), Shoe making, Soap production, Solar Energy, Photography and Editing, Paint production.
What's the duration of the program?
Answer: The duration of the program varies depending on the specific skill or course, but typically from a four weeks.

Are the skills Certified?
Answer: Yes, upon successful completion of the program, participants will receive a recognized certification for the skills they have acquired. and the best are empowered financially or tool/equipments to be used in the field.
